Welcome to MeGUI Website.

MeGUI is the most comprehensive GUI based ISO MPEG-4 solution. It suports MPEG-4 ASP (xvid), MPEG-4 AVC (x264), MPEG-H HEVC (x265) as video compressors and AAC, MP2, MP3, Flac, Vorbis, AC3 as audio compressors and various common containers support (MP4, MKV, AVI, M2TS).
